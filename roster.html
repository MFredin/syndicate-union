Rank Structure

Earn your rank

Ranks are earned through event participation and community contribution, not tenure alone. Ask an officer about your current standing any time.

Rank Requirements Perks
Recruit Joined the Discord, completed onboarding Access to general channels and guide library
Veteran 30+ days active, attended 3+ events Event voice priority, veteran-only channel
Officer Nominated by command staff for consistent leadership Can host events, moderate channels, mentor recruits
Vice Commander Appointed by the Commander to lead a division Full event planning access, syndicate diplomacy input
Commander Founding leadership Final say on server direction and partnerships
