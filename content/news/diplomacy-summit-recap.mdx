Representatives from eleven player organizations gathered for this quarter's Frontier Council session. Syndicate Union's delegation, led by Chief Diplomat S. Linwood, presented our position on shared access to the newly discovered trade lanes along the outer reach.

## Our position

We proposed a rotating access schedule that gives every signatory organization guaranteed windows, rather than a first-come model that favors whoever is online most. Six of eleven organizations co-sponsored the proposal.

## What happens next

The proposal moves to a formal vote at next quarter's session. In the meantime, the current informal arrangement holds, and no incidents have been reported along the disputed lanes.
