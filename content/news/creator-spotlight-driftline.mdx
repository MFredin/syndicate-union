Every month, Media & Culture spotlights a Unionist whose creative work has stood out to the community. This month, that honor goes to **Driftline**, whose orbital cityscape series now anchors our Gallery page.

## The series

Driftline's pieces reimagine the frontier's outposts as living cities — layered skylines, transit rings, and quiet details that reward a second look. Six pieces from the series are currently featured in the Gallery.

## In their own words

"I wanted to paint the frontier the way it feels between the fighting — busy, a little chaotic, but *lived in*. Every outpost has people who call it home."

Check out the full series on the Gallery page, and give Driftline a follow — links are on their profile card in the Member Directory.
