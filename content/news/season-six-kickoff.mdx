Season 6 is live, and it's the biggest shake-up the frontier has seen since launch. Here's what Fleet Command and Research & Industry want every Unionist to know before jumping in.

## The new sector map

Three new contested regions have opened along the outer reach, each with distinct resource profiles. Early scouting reports suggest the northern reach favors industry-focused syndicates, while the southern belt rewards aggressive fleet postures.

## Syndicate war rework

The war system now weights sustained map control over single decisive battles. Practically, this means:

- Shorter, more frequent skirmishes replace long siege windows
- Territory flips are contestable for 24 hours before finalizing
- Alliance treaties now factor into war eligibility

## What this means for your ranking

Rankings reset at season start, but veteran bonuses carry over for members with two or more full seasons of activity. Fleet Command will publish updated formation guides this week — keep an eye on the Guides library.
