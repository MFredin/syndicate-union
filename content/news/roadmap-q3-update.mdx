Transparency is one of the ways we try to live out the Union's values, so each quarter we publish an update to the public roadmap. Here's what's moving into active development this quarter.

## Now in progress

- **Department application workflow** — a proper multi-step flow for applying to a specific department, replacing the current ad-hoc form
- **Discord bot integration** — syncing roles, events, and announcements between the website and Discord automatically

## Recently completed

- Member directory with searchable badges and department filters
- Public wiki with structured categories and search
- Full events calendar with automatic timezone conversion

See the full Roadmap page for status on everything else in the pipeline, including the Creator Program and the allied organization portal.
