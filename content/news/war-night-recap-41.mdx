War Night 41 tested every squadron we had. A rival syndicate committed to a full push on our outer outpost, and Fleet Command answered with the largest coordinated defense of the season so far.

## The setup

Three fleets — Vantage's Wing, Corvine's tactical group, and a volunteer relief squadron — split coverage across the outpost's three approach vectors. Ground control estimated six incoming waves over ninety minutes.

## What worked

- **Staggered relief rotations** kept fresh pilots in the fight without ever leaving a vector uncovered
- **Real-time formation calls** in voice let squadron leads adapt mid-engagement
- **Pre-staged repair drones** cut our downtime between waves by more than half

## What we're taking into War Night 42

Fleet Command is adjusting the standard defense doctrine to formalize the staggered rotation approach — full write-up coming to the Guides library this week.

Thank you to every pilot who showed up. The outpost held because you did.
