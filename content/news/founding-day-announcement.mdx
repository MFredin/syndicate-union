Mark your calendars: Founding Day returns on **August 9th**, marking another year since a small group of commanders drafted the Union Charter and opened our doors.

## What to expect

- A community game night across several voice channels
- Giveaways contributed by our creator community
- A retrospective on the past year, presented by the Council of Elders
- The unveiling of this year's Founding Day badge

## How to join

No sign-up required — just show up in `#founding-day` on Discord when the event goes live. See the Events page for the exact time in your local timezone.

See you there, Unionists.
