New to Syndicate Union — or new to Foundation: Galactic Frontier entirely? Recruitment & Onboarding just shipped a refreshed version of the New Unionist Roadmap, and it's more specific than ever.

## Why a day-by-day list

Feedback from onboarding sessions kept pointing to the same problem: general advice like "focus on your economy early" doesn't tell you *what to actually do on Tuesday*. The updated roadmap fixes that with concrete daily priorities for your first two weeks.

## What's inside

1. **Days 1–3**: account setup, Discord orientation, first mentor session
2. **Days 4–7**: core economy loop and your first guide reading list
3. **Days 8–11**: joining your first low-stakes fleet operation
4. **Days 12–14**: choosing a department and submitting your first contribution

Find the full roadmap on the Guides page, and grab the printable PDF from Downloads if you'd rather work from paper.
