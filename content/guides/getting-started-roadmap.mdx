Welcome aboard. This roadmap is deliberately specific — instead of general advice, it tells you what to actually prioritize each day.

## Days 1–3: Get oriented

- Finish Discord onboarding and introduce yourself in `#arrivals`
- Book your first mentor session through Recruitment & Onboarding
- Read the About and Lore pages so the Union's values and history make sense

## Days 4–7: Learn the core loop

- Work through the Economy Fundamentals guide
- Sit in on one Fleet Command operation, even just to observe
- Start a reading list from the Wiki's "Foundations" category

## Days 8–11: Get involved

- Join a low-stakes fleet operation with your mentor's squad
- Attend a Monthly Strategy Roundtable if the timing lines up
- Ask questions — genuinely, there's no such thing as a bad one here

## Days 12–14: Choose your path

- Review the Recruitment page's department descriptions
- Talk to your mentor about which department fits your playstyle
- Submit your first contribution, however small — a guide edit, a gallery piece, or just showing up to a second operation

By day 14, you should have a mentor, a rough sense of where you fit, and at least one Union activity under your belt. Everything after that is up to you.
