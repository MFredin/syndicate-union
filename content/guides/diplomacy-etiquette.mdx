Every Unionist represents the Union, whether or not you're in the Diplomatic Corps. Here's how to handle contact with other syndicates in a way that keeps SU's reputation intact.

## The basics

- **Never negotiate on the Union's behalf** — pass any formal proposal to the Diplomatic Corps via `#diplomacy-referrals`
- **Stay honest in casual contact** — you can talk to rival syndicate members without representing an official Union position
- **Report hostile contact** — threats or harassment from other syndicates should go straight to Council, not handled one-on-one

## Why this matters

The Union's alliance network exists because past commanders took diplomacy seriously, even in casual interactions. A careless comment in a public channel can complicate a treaty the Diplomatic Corps spent weeks negotiating.

## If you're unsure

When in doubt, don't commit to anything — just say you'll pass the message along, and do. The Diplomatic Corps would rather field an unnecessary referral than clean up an unauthorized promise.
