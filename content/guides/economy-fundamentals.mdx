Before fleets, before diplomacy, before anything else — get your economy loop stable. Everything downstream depends on it.

## Priority order

1. **Raw extraction** — secure at least two extraction sites before expanding your base footprint
2. **Refinement capacity** — build refinement before you have a surplus, not after
3. **Storage buffer** — keep at least three days of buffer on your top two resources
4. **Trade routes** — only after the first three are stable

## Common early mistakes

- Overbuilding refinement before extraction can keep up with it
- Ignoring storage caps until a production stall wastes hours of output
- Chasing trade income before the home economy is self-sufficient

## When to ask for help

If your production chain feels stuck for more than a session or two, post your setup in `#economy-help` — Research & Industry analysts review requests daily.
