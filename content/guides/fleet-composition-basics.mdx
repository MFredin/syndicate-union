A bigger fleet isn't always a stronger one. Fleet Command's standard doctrine prioritizes role balance over headcount.

## The standard squadron

A baseline squadron includes:

- **2–3 vanguard** — absorb the opening engagement
- **3–4 strike** — carry the squadron's damage output
- **1–2 support** — repair, shield, and sustain the formation
- **1 tactical officer** — calls formation changes and targets

## Scaling up

When combining squadrons into a full fleet, keep the same ratios per squadron rather than stacking all vanguard together — a fleet of five all-vanguard squadrons folds faster than three balanced ones.

## Reading the field

Before committing to an engagement, a tactical officer should confirm:

1. Relative fleet size (not just visible ships — check for reinforcements)
2. Terrain or hazard modifiers in the contested zone
3. Whether support ships have line of sight to the vanguard
