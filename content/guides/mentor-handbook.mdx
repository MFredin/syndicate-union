Thank you for volunteering to mentor. This handbook covers what Recruitment & Onboarding expects and how to make the experience good for both of you.

## Your first session

- Confirm your mentee has finished basic Discord onboarding before you start
- Walk through the New Unionist Roadmap together — don't just link it
- Answer the "dumb questions" seriously; there aren't any

## Ongoing mentorship

Most mentor relationships run 2–4 weeks actively, then taper into casual check-ins. Aim for:

- One structured session in week one
- Light-touch availability for questions in weeks two and three
- A final check-in around week four to help them pick a department

## When it's not a good fit

Sometimes a mentor-mentee pairing just doesn't click, and that's fine. Let Recruitment & Onboarding know and we'll re-pair without making it a whole thing.

## Getting credit

Mentors who onboard five or more new Unionists earn the Mentor badge — Recruitment & Onboarding tracks this automatically.
