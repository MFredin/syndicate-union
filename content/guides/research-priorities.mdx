With three new contested regions live, research priorities have shifted from previous seasons. Here's the current recommendation from Research & Industry.

## Early priorities

1. **Extraction efficiency** — the new northern reach rewards industry-focused syndicates disproportionately
2. **Hull integrity** — the southern belt's aggressive fleet activity makes early survivability research pay off fast
3. **Sensor range** — critical for spotting the war system's shorter, more frequent skirmishes before they arrive

## Mid-game pivot

Once your economy loop is stable and initial defense research is complete, pivot toward whichever specialization matches your department's focus — Fleet Command pilots should prioritize weapons and mobility, while economy-focused members should continue down extraction and refinement lines.

## A note on respecs

Research respecs remain expensive this season. Confirm your priorities against your department's current goals before committing points — ask in `#research-help` if you're unsure.
