Season 6's new sector map can be overwhelming at first glance. Here's the short version.

## The three new regions

- **Northern Reach** — favors industry-focused syndicates; high extraction yield, low natural defense
- **Southern Belt** — rewards aggressive fleet postures; contested constantly, high risk and high reward
- **Central Corridor** — a connective trade zone; low resource yield but strategically important for logistics

## Choosing where to operate

New Unionists should generally avoid the Southern Belt until comfortable with Fleet Composition Basics — the contest frequency there punishes disorganized squads quickly. The Northern Reach is a safer training ground for new economy-focused members.

## Keeping current

The sector map shifts slightly each patch. Research & Industry posts updated maps in `#sector-intel` whenever significant changes land — check there before committing to a long-term base location.
