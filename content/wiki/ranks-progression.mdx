Syndicate Union uses a simple rank structure tied to tenure and contribution rather than in-game power.

## Standard ranks

1. **New Unionist** — first 14 days, paired with a mentor
2. **Unionist** — full member, eligible to join any department
3. **Senior Unionist** — six months tenure or a department lead nomination
4. **Veteran** — one year tenure with sustained department contribution

## Badges vs. ranks

Ranks track tenure; badges (see the Member Directory) track specific achievements like `Founding Unionist` or `War Veteran`. A member can hold any combination of badges regardless of rank.

## Department leadership

Leading a department is a separate track from standard ranks — leads are appointed by the Council based on contribution, not simply tenure.
