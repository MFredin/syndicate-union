## Primary resources

- **Raw ore** — extracted directly, feeds refinement chains
- **Refined alloy** — produced from raw ore, used in ship and structure construction
- **Volatile compounds** — extracted from contested zones, required for advanced research
- **Data cores** — recovered from exploration, used for research point generation

## Regional availability

Resource density varies by sector map region — see [Reading the Season 6 Sector Map](/guides/reading-the-sector-map) in the Guides library for the current season's specifics.

## Storage considerations

Every resource type has a storage cap that scales with structure tier. Production stalls are one of the most common early-game mistakes — see [Economy Fundamentals](/guides/economy-fundamentals) for buffer recommendations.
