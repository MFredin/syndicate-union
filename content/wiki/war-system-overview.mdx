## Core mechanics

Territory control is now determined by sustained presence rather than single decisive battles. Key rules:

- Skirmishes are shorter and more frequent than in previous seasons
- Territory flips remain contestable for 24 hours before finalizing
- Active alliance treaties factor into war eligibility between syndicates

## Strategic implications

Sustained, well-rested squadrons now outperform occasional large pushes. See [War Night Doctrine](/guides/war-night-doctrine) for the staggered rotation approach Fleet Command uses to sustain presence over long engagements.
