## The four classes

- **Vanguard** — high durability, absorbs opening engagement damage
- **Strike** — high damage output, the squadron's primary offense
- **Support** — repair and shield systems, sustains the formation
- **Scout** — high mobility and sensor range, used for reconnaissance

## Doctrine role

Fleet Command's standard squadron composition combines all four classes in set ratios — see [Fleet Composition Basics](/guides/fleet-composition-basics) in the Guides library for the current recommended ratios.
