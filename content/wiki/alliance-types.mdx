## Non-aggression pact

The most common agreement — both organizations commit to not engaging each other's forces, without further obligations.

## Cooperative agreement

Includes non-aggression plus coordination on specific goals, such as shared trade lane access or joint operations against a common threat.

## Formal alliance

The deepest tier, including mutual defense commitments. Syndicate Union currently holds no formal alliances of this type, preferring the flexibility of cooperative agreements.
