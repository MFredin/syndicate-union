## What trade routes are

Established paths between sectors that reduce transport risk and cost compared to ad-hoc shipping. Several key routes cross contested territory and require diplomatic agreements for safe passage.

## Shared trade lanes

The newly discovered outer-reach trade lanes are currently under an informal shared-access arrangement among allied organizations, pending a formal vote at the next Frontier Council session. See the News page for the latest diplomacy updates.

## Setting up your own route

New commanders should wait until their home economy is self-sufficient before investing in trade infrastructure — see [Economy Fundamentals](/guides/economy-fundamentals) for the recommended priority order.
