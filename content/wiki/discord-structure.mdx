## Top-level categories

- **Welcome** — `#arrivals`, `#rules`, onboarding channels
- **Community** — general chat, `#founding-day`, creator channels
- **Departments** — a category per department with public and internal channels
- **Operations** — Fleet Command's coordination and voice channels
- **Support** — `#website-feedback`, `#economy-help`, `#research-help`

## Voice channels

Named to match in-world locations referenced across the site — "Bridge Voice" for operations, "Council Chamber" for governance sessions, "Common Hall" for social events.

## Getting access

New members receive access to Welcome and Community categories immediately. Department channels unlock once you join that department through Recruitment & Onboarding.
