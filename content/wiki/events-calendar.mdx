## Recurring events

- **War Nights** — Fleet Command operations, roughly weekly
- **Onboarding Sessions** — for new Unionists, twice monthly
- **Strategy Roundtables** — Research & Industry, monthly
- **Social Nights** — Media & Culture, biweekly

## Timezone handling

All events are stored in UTC and converted automatically to your local timezone on the Events page — no manual conversion required.

## RSVPs

RSVPs help departments plan headcount but are never mandatory. See the Events page for how to RSVP and add events to your personal calendar.
