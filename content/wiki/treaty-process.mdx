## Steps

1. **Initial contact** — handled exclusively by the Diplomatic Corps; members should refer any external contact via `#diplomacy-referrals`
2. **Drafting** — terms negotiated and drafted by assigned envoys
3. **Council review** — the Council of Elders reviews and may request changes
4. **Ratification vote** — a simple majority Council vote ratifies the treaty
5. **Publication** — ratified treaties are summarized publicly; full text is available to members

## Amendments and termination

Either party may propose amendments at any time. Termination requires 14 days' notice except in cases of a material breach, which the Council may act on immediately.
