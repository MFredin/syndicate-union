The Union Charter is Syndicate Union's founding document, drafted at the Union's inception and amended only by a two-thirds Council vote.

## The five values

- **Liberty** — no member is pressured into a playstyle that doesn't suit them
- **Unity** — the syndicate comes before any individual's standing, including leadership
- **Honour** — commitments to allies and members are kept even when inconvenient
- **Progress** — the Union builds things meant to outlast the current season
- **Service** — leadership is a responsibility to the membership, not a reward

## Governance

The Charter establishes the Council of Elders as the Union's governing body, with one elected representative per department plus an elected Chancellor. See [Departments Overview](/wiki/departments-overview) for how department leadership relates to Council seats.

## Amendments

Amending the Charter requires a two-thirds majority vote of the Council of Elders, followed by a 14-day public comment period before ratification.
