Syndicate Union organizes its work across six departments. Full descriptions and open positions live on the Recruitment page — this is the quick reference version.

| Department | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| Fleet Command | Operations, war nights, defense doctrine |
| Diplomatic Corps | Alliances, treaties, external relations |
| Research & Industry | Guides, wiki, economy and research strategy |
| Recruitment & Onboarding | New member intake and mentorship |
| Media & Culture | Events, gallery, news dispatches |
| Council of Elders | Union-wide governance |

Each department (except Council) is led by a member appointed based on demonstrated contribution. Department leads sit on the Council alongside the elected Chancellor.
