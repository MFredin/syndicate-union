## Core terms

- **Vector** — a specific approach or defense line within an operation
- **Rotation** — a scheduled swap between active and resting squadrons
- **Fold** — when a formation collapses under pressure
- **Rally point** — a designated regroup location mid-engagement

## Why this matters

Shared terminology keeps voice calls short during live engagements. New pilots are encouraged to review this list before their first operation — Fleet Command calls move fast and won't pause to explain terms mid-fight.
