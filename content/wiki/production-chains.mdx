## The standard chain

Raw ore → Refined alloy → Components → Finished ships/structures

Each stage requires dedicated structure capacity, and bottlenecks at any stage cascade downstream.

## Common bottlenecks

- **Refinement lag** — extraction outpacing refinement capacity
- **Component queues** — manufacturing capacity outpaced by component demand during fleet buildup
- **Storage overflow** — any stage capping out and halting the stage before it

## Optimization tips

Research & Industry recommends auditing your production chain weekly during active buildup periods, not just when a stall is already visible.
