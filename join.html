How It Works

Three steps to get set up

Join the Discord

Click the invite above and accept the server rules to get past onboarding.

Pick your roles

Head to #role-select and choose your syndicate preference, timezone, and notification roles so the right channels light up.

Say hello and get matched

Introduce yourself in #new-commanders — an officer will match you into an active syndicate within a day or two.

Before You Join

Community Rules (Summary)

The full rules are pinned in Discord. The short version:

01

Respect First

No harassment, hate speech, or targeted negativity. Disagreements happen — keep them civil.

02

No Real-World Trading

Account sales, RMT, and boosting-for-cash offers aren't allowed anywhere on the server.

03

Keep It On-Topic

General chat is flexible, but strategy and event channels should stay focused on the game.

Still have questions?

Check the FAQ, or just ask in Discord once you're in — someone will point you in the right direction.
