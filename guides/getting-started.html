Getting Started: Your First 14 Days

Updated Jun 28, 2026 · Guides Team

New to Foundation: Galactic Frontier? This is the order we recommend to every recruit who joins Syndicate Union. It's built around avoiding the two most common early mistakes: over-investing in defense too early, and neglecting research queue uptime.

Days 1–3: Foundation Setup

  • Rush your first two production buildings before touching decoration or cosmetic upgrades.
  • Queue research constantly — an idle research queue is wasted time you can't get back.
  • Join a syndicate immediately. Solo play in the early game is strictly slower.

Days 4–7: Colony Expansion

  • Claim your second colony slot as soon as it's unlocked — don't wait for it to feel "ready."
  • Start a light escort fleet. You don't need a war fleet yet, just enough to deter scouts.
  • Use your daily free resource crates — they're easy to forget and add up fast.

Days 8–14: First Fleet Decisions

  • Pick a fleet identity (skirmish, siege, or support) rather than spreading resources evenly.
  • Start attending your syndicate's scheduled events — this is where most strategy knowledge actually transfers.
  • Read the Combat Basics guide before your first outpost contest.

Common Early Mistakes

  • Overbuilding defenses before you have anything worth defending.
  • Hoarding resources instead of spending them — nothing is saved by sitting on a full storage cap.
  • Skipping syndicate chat — most "hidden" mechanics get explained there long before they hit a wiki.
