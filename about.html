About the Union

Built by commanders, for commanders

Syndicate Union started as a handful of players trading tips in DMs. It's grown into one of the most active Foundation: Galactic Frontier communities around — and we're still commanded by the same principle: nobody defends a sector alone.

Our Mission

Make the frontier less overwhelming

Foundation: Galactic Frontier rewards players who understand its systems — but the learning curve is steep, and the good information is scattered. Syndicate Union exists to fix that: one place for guides, one place for events, one place to find people who'll actually answer your questions.

We're not affiliated with the game's publisher. We're players, same as you, who got tired of relearning the same lessons the hard way and decided to write it all down instead.

How We Got Here

  • Season 1 — A 40-person Discord server for one alliance's internal strategy notes.
  • Season 3 — Opened to the public after other alliances kept asking to see our guides.
  • Season 4 — Passed 1,000 members; stood up dedicated event and guide teams.
  • Season 6 (now) — 2,400+ commanders, 14 partnered syndicates, and a guide library covering every major system in the game.
